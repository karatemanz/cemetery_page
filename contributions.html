Rehoboth Cemetery Association

Contributions:

Memorial Contributions are always welcome at Rehoboth Cemetery. Through generous contributions relatives and loved ones of interments, we have been able to perform many upgrades to the infrastructure and improve the financial viability of Rehoboth Cemetery. Contributions can be dedicated in the memory of a loved ones and relatives. A brass plaque is created for each contribution and is placed on the Memorial Board that hangs on the wall in the Chapel. Contributions can be made in the form of cash, check or money order to Rehoboth Cemetery Association.
We would like to meet you in person but if that is not possible please send contributions to Rehoboth Cemetery Association at 441 Rehoboth Church Road P.O. Box 253 Belle Vernon PA, 15012
